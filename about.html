About Us

At Sweet & Bakers, we believe in delivering more than just desserts — we deliver smiles, moments, and memories.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Sweet & Bakers started as a small home bakery with a big dream: to bring joy through hand-crafted desserts. From cupcakes to custom gift hampers, every item is made with love, using the finest ingredients.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of customers across the globe — thanks to our passionate team and loyal customers.
